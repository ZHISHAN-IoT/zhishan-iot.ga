To create the world-leading touch and driver ICdesign company,FocalTech merges Orise at 11% premium.

Time:2014-04-08    From:FocalTech

FocalTechCorporationLtd.,(hereinafter“FocalTech”, 5280. TW)and Orise Technology Corp.(hereinafterOrise, 3545. TW)announced today, 2014 Apr 7,that both Board of Directors approved theMerger and Share Swap plan with each share of FocalTech swapping4.8 newly issued shares of Orise.FocalTech will maintain the management control of thenew companyafter merge. The effectivedate of the Merger and Share Swapis proposedon January 2, 2015. The original major shareholder of Orise,Sunplus Technology Co., Ltd. will become a pure equity investor.In light of both closing prices one day before the announcement day, the premium rate which FocalTech offersto Orise is approximately 11%.

 FocalTech is the largest touch controller IC providerin Asia, with market share over 50%in China.The company has a complete line of capacitivetouch controller products ranging from small handheld device up to Notebook PCand with wide portfolio of patents.FocalTechis a technology leader in on-cell and in-cell touch technologies and is recently known as the fastest growing IC company in Asia.Orise Technology specializes in the research, development, design, and manufacture of Flat Panel Display Driver IC and Flat Panel Display Controller IC. Orise Technology not only masters the most advanced design technologies, as well as up-to-date IP, but also focuses on the innovation of core technology, providing customers with thebest solutions, and meeting every singular requirement from customers with the highest standard of service.

The migration of laminated touch panels towards on-cell and in-cell also calls for the integration of touch controller and display driver IC’s. With the complementary products and talents from FocalTechand Orise, the new merged company can benefit from the scale of economy; not only with wider product lines, broadermarket, but the potential R&D capability of developing new and more powerful product better and faster. 

Genda Hu, Chairmanof FocalTech,said "Orisehasstrong and complete product linein display drivers whileFocalTech is the No. 1touch IC provider in Asia.This merge of two companies is like a marriagemade in heaven. "

FocalTech Corporation, Ltd is an IC design house founded in 2005, specialized in designing, manufacturing and marketing of touch controller IC. FocalTech has successfully demonstrated full capability to support customer demands in large volume production and with highest levels of reliability for the past years. Nowadays, FocalTech becomes a world’s leading touch panel controller IC supplier, serving many international top tier mobile or handheld brands. In 2013, FocalTech shipment exceeds 200 Million units world-wide.

Orise Technology, which officially became independent of Sunplus Technology in 2006 April, was established by a group of excellent TFT project engineers in 2006 January. Now Orise Technology is headquartered in Taiwan Hsinchu Science Based Industry Park and has been a listed company on the Taiwan Stock Exchange Corporate since 2007 July. Main products include: Large-sized TFT Panel Driver IC, Timing Controller IC, Medium/Small-sized TFT Panel Controller and Driver SOC, STN Panel Driver IC and the relative ASIC.
