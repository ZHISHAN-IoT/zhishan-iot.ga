Silicon Labs Acquires Wi-Fi Innovator Zentri

-- Zentri Expands Silicon Labs' IoT Connectivity Portfolio --

AUSTIN, Texas, Jan. 23, 2017 /PRNewswire/ -- Silicon Labs (NASDAQ: SLAB), a leading supplier of silicon, software and solutions for a more connected world, announces the acquisition of Zentri, an innovator in low-power, cloud-connected Wi-Fi technologies for the Internet of Things (IoT). Zentri helps customers worldwide securely connect and manage their products across a wide range of industrial, commercial and consumer applications.

Zentri's unique combination of modules, embedded and cloud software, APIs and tools enables rapid development of secure IoT end node products in a matter of weeks. By eliminating the need for wireless design expertise and providing a library of cloud-connected applications, Zentri allows IoT device makers to focus on differentiating their products and speeding time to market.

"Silicon Labs is proud to join forces with Zentri," said Tyson Tuttle, CEO of Silicon Labs. "This acquisition builds upon our strategy to deliver comprehensive, multiprotocol silicon and software solutions to securely connect IoT products to the cloud. Zentri's Wi-Fi expertise, software and tools will extend our multiprotocol connectivity portfolio to a wider range of power-sensitive IoT end node applications."

The global IT research firm Gartner recognized Zentri in its "Cool Vendors in IoT 'Thingification' 2016" report.

About Zentri
Zentri is an innovator in secure, connected solutions for the IoT, transforming the way companies develop secure mobile and cloud-connected products. Leveraging Zentri's hardware, software, APIs and tools, companies can rapidly develop and deliver highly secure and robust products to market in a matter of weeks. Zentri enables secure product authentication, seamless over-the-air (OTA) software updates, remote product diagnosis and analytics, and more. Learn more at www.zentri.com.
